Chicken Alfredo

Description

This is a really basic recipe for chicken alfredo! Note this recipe calls for store brought sauce and noodles, but feel free to make your own at home!
This recipe is quite light on both kitchen utensils and overall effort - you'll just need a large pot, a pan, and a spoon or spatula! Feel free to make large portions as well - this alfredo keeps great for a few days in the fridge!

Ingredient List

  • Alfredo noodles (or any pasta noodle you wish)
  • Chicken Breast, cubed
  • Garlic Alfredo sauce
  • Heavy whipping cream (or half and half)
  • Salt and Pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Bring a pot of water to a boil, and add your pasta. Let simmer until al dente - roughly 13 minutes.
  2. Add your desired seasoning blend to the chicken breast
  3. Add some oil to the pan, and add your chicken. Pan fry until cooked through with desired browning - roughly 5 minutes over medium-high
  4. Set aside your chicken and, into the same pan, add your garlic alfredo sauce and your desired amount of heavy cream - adjusting the latter as needed for thickness
  5. Keep the sauce at a gentle simmer, then drain your cooked pasta noodles
  6. Stir in your pasta into the sauce
  7. Top your pasta with the chicken cooked previously
